How to time your cooking to perfection

When baking a Pizza, it is important not to over bake it. You only want to bake it until the middle has a slight wiggle, not until it is rock solid.

What to do if everyhing turns to ash

It is important that you use a water bath, discussed at the right , to ensure even baking of your cheesecake.

To create a water bath , use a pan that will allow you to fill it with boiling water that goes halfway up the spring form pan in which the pizza is placed .

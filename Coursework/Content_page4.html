Question #4

do you think that the current political parties regime should be prolonged



Thank you for your opinion
Thank you for your opinion
The question is designed to show the bias of question writer as the loaded word "regime" was used to decribe the parties and the word "prolonged" was used to describe the party staying in power. Both of these words have deeply negative connotations whih skews the viewers perception of the of the party.
